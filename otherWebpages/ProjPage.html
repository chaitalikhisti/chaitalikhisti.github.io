Research Projects

Replication as a Fault Tolerance Technique in Database Systems

Context: Database Management Systems, Fault Tolerant Computing

  • All relational and non-relational databases implement a replication mechanism for data recovery in case of faults and failures to increase system reliability and availability.
  • This case study aims at studying these mechanisms in various database systems along with their challenges to determine the robust and most suitable replication metod.

3D Networks-On-Chip Architectures

Context: Design of Embedded Systems

  • To meet the increasing demands of semiconductor industry, developing NoCs with 3D architecture proves to be a promising approach.
  • This case study focuses on studying various topologies and architectures to implement this approach along with its functionalities and applications.
  • The conclusion thus reached is that the selection for 3D NoC depends on the trade-off consideration between performance and power requirements of the structure which is application specific.

London Underground: An Engineered System

Context: Systems Engineering

  • The case study explores the system engineering principles applied to the successful implementation of London Underground (also known as Tube) system.
  • A detailed study of the concept development, engineering stage and post development helps to understand the specifications and real time implementation of this complex system.
